Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

Privacy First: PicToPDF processes all your documents locally on your device. Your files never leave your phone, ensuring complete privacy and security.

Information We Collect

PicToPDF is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information:

  • No Personal Documents: Your photos and PDFs are processed entirely on your device
  • No Account Required: You can use the app without creating an account
  • Basic Analytics: Anonymous usage statistics to improve the app (via Google Analytics)
  • Crash Reports: Anonymous crash data to fix bugs and improve stability

How We Use Information

The limited information we collect is used only to:

  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Fix bugs and technical issues
  • Understand which features are most useful
  • Show relevant advertisements (via Google AdMob)

Data Storage

All your documents and images are stored locally on your device in the app's private storage area. This means:

  • Only you have access to your files
  • Files are not backed up to any cloud service
  • Uninstalling the app will delete all your data
  • Other apps cannot access your PicToPDF files

Third-Party Services

PicToPDF uses these trusted third-party services:

  • Google ML Kit: For document scanning (processing happens on-device)
  • Google AdMob: For displaying advertisements
  • Google Analytics: For anonymous usage statistics

Permissions

PicToPDF requests only essential permissions:

  • Camera: To capture document photos
  • Storage: To save PDFs and access your photos (via file picker)
  • Internet: Only for ads and analytics (optional)

Children's Privacy

PicToPDF is safe for users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app works entirely offline and doesn't require any personal information.

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy occasionally. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
